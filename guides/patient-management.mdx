## Overview

The patient management system in SANL allows healthcare providers to maintain comprehensive digital health records for nutritional monitoring. Built on a modern stack with Supabase database and Next.js frontend, the platform provides secure, real-time access to patient information.

<Note>
  All patient data is stored securely in Supabase with row-level security policies to ensure HIPAA compliance and data privacy.
</Note>

## Creating a New Patient Profile

Follow these steps to register a new patient in the system:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Patient Registration">
    Navigate to the **Patients** section from the main dashboard and click the **Add New Patient** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Basic Information">
    Complete the required patient details:
    
    - Full name
    - Date of birth
    - Contact information (email, phone)
    - Emergency contact details
    
    <Tip>
      Use the patient's preferred contact method to ensure effective follow-up communication.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Medical History">
    Document the patient's medical background:
    
    - Current medications
    - Known allergies
    - Chronic conditions
    - Previous surgeries or major health events
    - Family medical history relevant to nutrition
    
    <Warning>
      Always verify allergy information before creating any nutrition plans. This data is automatically referenced during plan creation.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record Initial Measurements">
    Capture baseline health metrics:
    
    - Height and weight
    - BMI (automatically calculated)
    - Blood pressure
    - Blood glucose levels
    - Other relevant biomarkers
    
    These measurements establish a baseline for tracking progress over time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save and Review">
    Click **Save Patient Profile**. The system will:
    
    - Validate all required fields
    - Store data securely in Supabase
    - Generate a unique patient ID
    - Create an initial clinical record entry
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Existing Patient Records

### Viewing Patient Information

Access comprehensive patient data through the patient dashboard:

1. Search for patients using the search bar (by name, ID, or email)
2. Click on a patient card to view their complete profile
3. Review tabs for different data categories:
   - **Overview**: Key metrics and recent activity
   - **Medical History**: Conditions, medications, allergies
   - **Measurements**: Historical health data with trend charts
   - **Nutrition Plans**: Active and past dietary interventions
   - **Reports**: Generated PDF reports and AI insights

### Updating Patient Records

Keep patient information current with regular updates:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-pen" title="Personal Information Updates">
    Edit contact details, emergency contacts, or demographic information:
    
    1. Open the patient profile
    2. Click **Edit Profile** in the top-right corner
    3. Modify the necessary fields
    4. Click **Save Changes**
    
    All changes are timestamped and logged in the system for audit purposes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="notes-medical" title="Medical History Updates">
    Add new medications, allergies, or conditions:
    
    1. Navigate to the **Medical History** tab
    2. Click **Add Entry** for the relevant category
    3. Enter details including:
       - Date of diagnosis or prescription
       - Severity or dosage
       - Prescribing physician (if applicable)
       - Notes or special considerations
    4. Save the entry
    
    <Note>
      The system will flag potential interactions between medications and nutrition plans automatically.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Recording New Measurements">
    Track patient progress by adding new health metrics:
    
    1. Go to the **Measurements** tab
    2. Click **Add Measurement**
    3. Select the measurement type or add multiple at once
    4. Enter values with the date and time
    5. Save the measurements
    
    The system automatically:
    - Calculates derived metrics (BMI, BMR)
    - Generates trend visualizations
    - Highlights significant changes from baseline
    - Flags values outside normal ranges
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Health Data Tracking

### Continuous Monitoring

SANL enables longitudinal tracking of patient health metrics with real-time data synchronization through Supabase:

**Key Tracked Metrics:**

- **Anthropometric Data**: Weight, height, BMI, body composition
- **Vital Signs**: Blood pressure, heart rate, temperature
- **Laboratory Results**: Blood glucose, cholesterol, HbA1c, vitamin levels
- **Dietary Compliance**: Meal adherence, calorie intake, macronutrient distribution
- **Activity Levels**: Exercise frequency, step counts, active minutes

## Clinical Record Management

### Session Notes

Document patient consultations and interventions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a New Session">
    From the patient profile, click **New Consultation** to begin documenting a session.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record Session Details">
    Document the consultation:
    
    - Chief complaints or reason for visit
    - Physical examination findings
    - Patient-reported dietary adherence
    - Discussion points and concerns
    - Clinical decisions made
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link Related Data">
    Associate relevant information:
    
    - Link to specific measurements taken during visit
    - Reference nutrition plan modifications
    - Attach lab results or external documents
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Visit Summary">
    Use the AI-powered summary feature:
    
    The system leverages Google Gemini AI to generate concise visit summaries from your session notes, highlighting key points and action items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Document Management

Manage patient documents and reports:

- **Upload External Files**: Lab results, referral letters, medical imaging reports
- **Generate PDF Reports**: Create comprehensive nutritional assessment reports using the built-in PDF generation system (powered by fpdf2)
- **Version Control**: Track document versions and maintain audit trails
- **Secure Sharing**: Share documents with patients or other providers with controlled access

## Patient Communication

### Appointment Management

Schedule and manage patient appointments:

1. Navigate to the **Calendar** view
2. Click on a time slot to create a new appointment
3. Select the patient from the dropdown
4. Set appointment type (initial consultation, follow-up, nutritional assessment)
5. Add appointment notes or preparation instructions
6. The system sends automatic email reminders to patients

<Tip>
  Set up recurring appointments for patients requiring regular monitoring to ensure consistent care.
</Tip>

### Messaging and Notifications

Stay connected with patients between visits:

- **In-app Messaging**: Send secure messages to patients about plan updates or reminders
- **Email Notifications**: Automated reminders for appointments, measurements, or plan milestones
- **Alert System**: Get notified when patients log critical measurements outside normal ranges

## Data Security and Privacy

SANL implements robust security measures to protect patient data:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shield-halved" title="Access Controls">
    - Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures users only see authorized data
    - Row-level security in Supabase prevents unauthorized database access
    - Multi-factor authentication available for enhanced security
    - Automatic session timeout after inactivity
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="lock" title="Data Encryption">
    - All data encrypted at rest in Supabase
    - TLS encryption for all data in transit
    - Secure API communication between Next.js frontend and FastAPI backend
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="clock-rotate-left" title="Audit Trails">
    - Complete logging of all data access and modifications
    - Timestamped record of who viewed or edited patient information
    - Compliance reporting available for regulatory requirements
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="exclamation-triangle" title="Patient record won't save">
    **Common causes:**
    
    - Missing required fields (check for red asterisks)
    - Invalid data format (e.g., incorrect date format)
    - Database connection issue (check your internet connection)
    - Session timeout (refresh the page and try again)
    
    If the problem persists, check the browser console for error messages or contact technical support.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="magnifying-glass" title="Can't find a patient">
    **Search tips:**
    
    - Try searching by patient ID instead of name
    - Check for spelling variations or typos
    - Use the advanced filter options to narrow results
    - Verify you have access permissions for that patient
    - Check if the patient record was archived
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Measurements not displaying in charts">
    **Troubleshooting steps:**
    
    1. Verify measurements were saved successfully
    2. Check the date range filter on the chart
    3. Ensure at least two data points exist for trend analysis
    4. Refresh the page to reload chart data
    5. Clear browser cache if charts appear frozen
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
